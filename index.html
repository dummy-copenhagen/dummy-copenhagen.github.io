dummy - SUPERGAMES
Tagensvej 126 - 30.08.2025
dummy's first edition of one day only burner shows SUPERGAMES, which took place at a former gas station and its premises, at Tagensvej 126. SUPERGAMES is inspired by the idea of public space as a fleeting time, relational and unfinished, a space shaped not only by rules, social hierarchies, and industrial infrastructures, but also open to play, intervention, appropriation, and embodied presence. Borrowing from Henri Lefebvre’s notion of “the right to the city”, a right to celebrate, challenge, and reimagine the urban, SUPERGAMES explores the ephemeral, the fleeting, and the ritualistic in the encounter between body, material, and place. 30th of August kl. 17-24 Participating Artists: Flora Aussant, Zahna Siham Benamor, Leïth Benkhedda, Erdal Bilici, Niels Christensen, Magnus Frederik Clausen & Anskar Beau, Lukas Danys, Simon Dybbroe Møller, Leo Elia, Frederik Exner, Julie Falk, Mishael Fapohunda, Toke Flyvholm, Fryd Frydendahl, Cyprien Gaillard, Kaspars Groševs, Rune Kielsgaard, Siri Hammarén, Maja Li Härdelin, Mark Leckey, Alva Le Febvre, Lars Mathisen, Dagmar Moldovanu, Mykhailyna Moskalenko, Aske Olsen, Mischa Pavlovski, Vio Rossi, Jan S.
